CARES

Chabot College's Annual Suicide Prevention Walk, Spring 2024

The purpose of this flyer was to attract students, staff, and the community to attend Chabot College’s CARES Annual Suicide Awareness Walk and Resource Fair. I ensured that all the necessary information was included and organized the flyer to align with the fair’s theme and color scheme. Made with Adobe illustrator



The Marias

Band concert poster, Spring 2024

Drawing inspiration from The Marías, a Pop-indie soft rock band, and their 2022 Cinema Tour, I created a concert poster that reflected the tour’s theme. The purpose of this poster was to attract fans by capturing the essence of the band's aesthetic. I made sure to make María, the band's lead singer, the central figure to highlight her importance in the band's overall image. Made with Adobe illustrator.


Film Festival

Chabot College's Annual Film Festival Poster Entry, Spring 2024

After taking a video editing class, I was inspired to use a film slate as the central element in my design for Chabot College’s Annual Film Festival poster. Since the festival is relatively new, I incorporated a color scheme based on the college’s official colors to create a sense of familiarity. The bold yellow background was chosen not only to reflect the school’s association but also to grab attention and stand out, drawing nearby viewers to the event. Made with Adobe illustrator.
